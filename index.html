Affogato

This is the perfect small dessert for any occasion. The extreme sweetness of the ice cream and the extreme bitterness of the espresso pair very well. When you add the bittersweet dark chocolate, the flavors melt together into a heavenly experience.



Ingredients





Ice Cream
Espresso
Shaved Dark Chocolate



C a u t i o n !

Contains: Dairy and Approx. 128mg Caffeine



Recipe


Brew your Espresso


Scoop your Ice Cream


Pour your Espresso over your Ice Cream


Garnish with your Chocolate Shavings

Enjoy!
